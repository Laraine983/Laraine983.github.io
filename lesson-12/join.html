Joining the Chamber of Commerce is a simple and effective way to bring awareness of your business to the community and helps other businesses as well.

Here are a few perks that members of the Chamber can enjoy:

1) Publicizing their business by listing it in our online business directory.
2) Business trainings from licensed professionals to help you find ways to improve advertising, customer service, income, etc.
3) Social media content created by a marketing professional to boost your engagement and recognition with customers.
4) Tickets to participate in luncheons.
5) Introduction to and meetings with investors and sponsors.

*Perks only available to certain membership levels

MEMBERSHIP KEY:

Gold: includes all perks listed above as well as many others.
Silver: Includes perks #1-4 listed above.
Bronze: Includes perks #1-2 listed above.
Non-profit: Includes perk #1 listed above.
