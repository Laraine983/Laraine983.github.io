About Denton


Denton has a population of 141,541 (https://www.census.gov/quickfacts/fact/table/dentoncitytexas/LND110210)
15, 826 of those are students at Texas Women's University (https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/texas-womans-university-3646)
39,192 of those are students at University of North Texas (https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/unt-3594)

Denton is home to two amazing universities - Texas Women's University (TWU) and the University of North Texas(UNT).
