About Denton


Population: 141,541


15,826 students at Texas Women's University


39,192 students at University of North Texas

Denton is home to two amazing universities - Texas Women's University (TWU) and the University of North Texas(UNT).

At the heart of Denton is the historic Courthouse on the Square. Surrounded by amazing restaurants, specialty shops, and other thriving businesses, the Square is a natural gathering place for all residents of Denton. Naturally, this is also home to many of Denton's best events.

Some upcoming events:

August 20-28: North Texas Fair & Rodeo

September 11: Arts & Autos Extravaganza

October 1-3: Denton Arts & Jazz Festival
