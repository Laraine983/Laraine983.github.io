About Denton


  • Founded April 11th, 1846
  • Population: 141,541
  • University Students: 55,018
  • Females: 51.2% , Males: 48.8%
  • Residents over age 18: 80.5%
  • Median income: $60,018

Denton is home to two amazing universities - Texas Woman's University (TWU) and the University of North Texas(UNT).

At the heart of Denton is the historic Courthouse on the Square. Surrounded by amazing restaurants, specialty shops, and other thriving businesses, the Square is a natural gathering place for all residents of Denton. Naturally, this is also home to many of Denton's best events, such as the annual Arts and Jass Festival.

Some upcoming events:

August 20-28: North Texas Fair & Rodeo

September 11: Arts & Autos Extravaganza

October 1-3: Denton Arts & Jazz Festival



We Love Our Veterans 🏅

Denton is the proud home of the Texas Veterans Hall of Fame museum, which recently got a new home in the Golden Triangle Mall. Visitors can hear the stories of our brave veterans and learn more about our history. For non-veterans, consider visiting the museum or viewing some of the online features in the virtual museum. Veterans who have served can share their stories and have them featured in the museum. Donations can be made to help preserve the museum, further the collection of stories and artifacts, and give back to our veterans.
For more information or to make a donation, visit the website: https://txveterans.org/
