Target audience:

The targeted audience for this website is those living in or visiting the 3 towns that will be displayed on the site.

Two Personas:


The first person is Jane, a mom with young kids. She is familiar with technology and regularly checks the weather to know whether or not she can take
her kids to the park or on other outdoor adventures. She usually looks on the weather app on her phone, but wants information more pertinent to
her exact town besides just the weather, without having to search multiple sources.
The second person is Gerald, a 65 year old man who checks the weather daily, but uses his computer to do so. He is not very familiar with technology
and wants to be able to see the forecast and other local events without having to navigate through a bunch of confusing web pages.
