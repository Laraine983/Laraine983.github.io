Design Principles

Hick's Law

Pinterest is a good example of keeping the choices simple. When I view a pin, I have a few options to choose from, such as saving, sharing, downloading, visiting the page, or searching.

Rule of Thirds

This website uses the rule of thirds well. None of the four icons are centered, making it more visually appealing and balanced. The rule of thirds can be a great tool to use for websites to help them feel more natural and appealing.

PARC: Proximity

The proximity of each item for sale on this page helps the reader to know what information belongs to which item. The text below each tablet is grouped closely to show the relationship between the items, such as the name, price, and other options.
